What is the difference between an Intel and AMD processor?
Intel processors are known for strong single-core performance, making them great for tasks like gaming and general use, while AMD processors excel in multi-core performance, offering better value for multitasking and productivity tasks like video editing. Both brands are reliable, but your choice depends on your specific needs and budget.
How much RAM do I need for my PC?
It depends on how much your priority is on multi-tasking. We generally suggest our customers to start from 8GB for meeting requirements of latest softwares. We can go upto 2TB RAM for servers which do not compromise on multi-tasking.
What graphics card should I choose for gaming or video editing?
For gaming, choose a graphics card like NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4060 or AMD Radeon RX 7600 for smooth performance at 1080p or 1440p. For video editing, prioritize cards with more VRAM, like the NVIDIA RTX 3060 (12GB) or higher, to handle rendering and 4K footage efficiently. Your choice depends on your budget and performance needs.
